Preserving the integrity of the Earth

We are a youth-led Climate Justice and air quality advocacy movement aimed at mobilizing the collective power of society to mitigative Climate Change.

Join the movement

Mobilization

We marshal youths and constantly beseech them to come together with one voice, hand in hand to make a change and say no to the global threat posed by climate change. To Speak, To Strike, To Support, and To Vote for climate.

Direct Action

Climate change is already hurting millions. Gray2green would be part of the global coalition of climate justice activists that campaign for a switch from fossil fuels to renewable and cleaner alternatives. We believe that health and future of our environment is in our hands thus, we vow to embrace positive behavioural change, engage practically on climate change mitigation and adaptation measures, Promote the preservation our forests through afforestation, Boycott product from organisations that lobby against climate legislation.

Education and Communication

Learning is inexplicable to humans. As we unravel new things about climate change and sustainable development, we expeditiously think of creative methods to relay our knowledge to the public through regular teach-in, seminars and conference, grooming of Students and organizing of hackathons and competitions that helps tackle the climate crisis.
