ABOUT US

Gray2Green Movement

We are a youth-led Climate Justice and air quality advocacy movement aimed at mobilizing the collective power of society to mitigative Climate Change.

Our Mission

To consolidate ourselves with the global climate justice campaign. We are providing a platform for uniting youth voices for positive change and to be part of the forces that drives real change.

Our Vision

We hope to achieve an inclusive, resilient and sustainable environment where humanity can live and carry out its primary and social function without putting the health of the earth in peril.

Our Focus

We are a Team, a Voice, and a People with a common goal and focus on bringing together a community of people with common concerns for climate justice and air quality.

Our Values

We are a youth-led movement
We will be peaceful and non-violent We will extend the hand of friendship
We will demand that our allies take action in solidarity with us
We will work to protect each other
We will not allow the system to divide us
We will sow seeds to protect our future
